Butcher Shop Website



Roles

Lead UX Designer , Researcher


Project Goals

Enable customers to shop from their local butcher's store online, allowing for greater flexability and time saving.


Target Audience

Busy parents and professionals who can't devote the time and energy required to go to the grocery store.


Constraints & Challenges

The main challenge was making sure the website met the unique needs of the user.

User Testing Results

We performed two rounds of remote, supervised usability testing, which garnered the following findings:


Round 1 findings

  1. Users Wanted more information on the order confirmation page.

Round 2 findings

  1. Users found the main checkout flow easy to follow.
  2. Users felt that the checkout process was a little long.
